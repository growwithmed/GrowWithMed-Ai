## What Is ClickFunnels?

ClickFunnels is the OG of funnel builders — launched in 2014 by Russell Brunson, it essentially invented the "sales funnel" category for online entrepreneurs. It's powerful, polished, and has one of the most active communities in the online business space.

ClickFunnels is built for marketers who want high-converting, drag-and-drop funnel pages with advanced features like:

- Multi-step funnels with upsells and downsells
- A/B split testing
- Webinar funnels
- A large library of proven templates
- Deep integration with third-party CRMs and email tools

### ClickFunnels Pricing

ClickFunnels starts at **$97/month** for the basic plan. Their ClickFunnels 2.0 suite goes up to $297/month for the full feature set. There is a 14-day free trial, but no permanent free tier.

For solopreneurs or beginners, that's a significant monthly commitment — especially before you've made your first dollar online.

## Where ClickFunnels Still Wins

Let's be honest — ClickFunnels isn't overpriced for nothing. There are real areas where it pulls ahead.

**1. Advanced A/B Split Testing**
ClickFunnels has more robust and granular A/B testing features, making it better for businesses that are optimizing funnels at scale and need statistically significant data fast.

**2. Larger Community and Ecosystem**
The ClickFunnels community is enormous — think active Facebook groups, FunnelHackers events, tons of third-party courses, and a massive library of shared funnel templates. If peer learning and networking matter to you, ClickFunnels wins.

**3. Deeper Third-Party Integrations**
ClickFunnels integrates natively with more CRMs, payment processors, and advanced marketing tools. If you're running a complex business stack with tools like Salesforce, HubSpot, or Infusionsoft, ClickFunnels will plug in more cleanly.
